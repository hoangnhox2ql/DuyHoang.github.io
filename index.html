FIRST AUTHOR PAPER
Multimodal Fuzzy Knowledge Graph Framework for Clinical Diagnosis
Integrating Imaging, Signal, and Tabular Data from Symile-MIMIC for the Diagnosis of Pleural Effusion. A novel Tri-modal Fuzzy Knowledge Graph (FKG) architecture integrating frontal chest radiographs (CXR), 12-lead ECG signals, and laboratory analytes.
  • Weighted feature fusion mechanism with optimal FKGS sampling algorithm
  • Reduced computational time nearly three-fold while maintaining interpretability
✦ Peak Accuracy: 82.05% — Pioneering Tri-modal Benchmark on Symile-MIMIC
FIRST AUTHOR PAPER EAI GoodTech Conference
Adaptive Weight Fusion for Multimodal Diabetic Retinopathy Diagnosis
Using Fuzzy Knowledge Graph Structure. A novel diagnostic system that significantly enhances the accuracy of Diabetic Retinopathy detection by intelligently fusing multimodal data — retinal images and clinical tabular data.
  • Adaptive Weight Fusion (AWF) method that dynamically calculates feature importance
  • Integrated into Fuzzy Knowledge Graph Structure (FKGS) for interpretable results
✦ Submitted to EAI GoodTech Conference
